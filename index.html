About Us

Bhartiya Kisan Union (Bhanu) is a grassroots-driven farmers’ movement that champions the cause of India’s agricultural community. Rooted in the spirit of unity, justice, and progress, BKU (Bhanu) represents millions of farmers and works relentlessly to amplify their voices at the state and national levels.

The union stands as a pillar of strength for farmers facing challenges like unfair market prices, outdated policies, and lack of government support. With a clear mission to protect the rights of annadatas (food providers), BKU (Bhanu) organizes peaceful protests, policy discussions, and awareness campaigns to drive meaningful change.

Rashtriya Mantri Amit Tyagi, a dynamic and influential leader, plays a crucial role in shaping the union’s strategies and outreach. Known for his commitment to farmer empowerment and national development, he brings passion, integrity, and experience to the movement.
