About Us

Amit Tyagi, serving as the BJP Jila Adhyaksh, is a dynamic and dedicated leader committed to the vision of Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas. With deep roots in grassroots politics and a strong connection with the people of his district, he has continuously worked towards empowering local communities and strengthening the BJP's organizational structure at the district level.

Under his leadership, the district has witnessed significant progress in areas such as rural development, youth engagement, women's empowerment, and social welfare. Amit Tyagi is known for his transparent working style, accessibility to the public, and ability to mobilize party workers with enthusiasm and discipline.

As Jila Adhyaksh, Amit Tyagi remains committed to carrying forward the BJP’s mission with honesty, dedication, and a strong vision for a self-reliant and empowered Bharat.
